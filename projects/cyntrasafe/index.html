CyntraSafe is a network monitoring server that regularly checks the integrity of your servers for intrusions

Through our dashboard you can view your server's diagnostics and quickly see if there are security risks that need to be addressed. Our trusted professionals are on standby 24/7 ready to tackle any intrusion of your network.

Not just monitoring

We actively take steps to improve and update your networks security. All modifications to your network are logged and can easily be reviewed or reverted if desired. Our coverage applies to all facets of your network: websites, cloud hosting, data centers or on-site hosting.

Control your notifications

You have full control over who gets notified for what. Alerts for SSL certificates, DDoS protection, server maintenance and more can all be toggled or only sent to specific team members. We understand the stress too many alerts can put on a security team so we classify priority on ALL of our alerts.
